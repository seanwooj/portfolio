One University Network

One University Network aims to build a platform with which international universities could get the courses and curriculum online simply and painlessly. To do so, they needed an intuitive mobile application for the student experience, such that the content could be delivered seamlessly to the end users. Because of limitations with data plans internationally, careful consideration was given to a variety of UX use cases to ensure that users could be have content delivered to them without having it cost them.

I was tasked with building out the initiual UX, from sketches to rough wireframes, and finally to high fidelity mockups. In addition to this, I built out interactive prototypes using pixate such that the development team could see how the interactions were intended to work. I was also tasked with defining the rough data model for courses within the platform.
