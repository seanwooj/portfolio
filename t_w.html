Tailor & White

Tailor & White is a high end youth hostel located in Budapest, Hungary. As the highest rated hostel in the country, and perhaps central and eastern Europe, a combination of good design and edginess are key to the continued success of the hostel. To that end, I was commissioned to create posters and totes for the hostel with their branding as well as to promote many of the cultural events that they put out.

A monotone design was important to the client and to that end, I stuck with black and white, to be consistent with the interior of the hostel itself, the decor of which is largely black and white as well. To attract an international, culturally aware clientele the imagery and typography was intentionally edgy and somewhat bleak.
