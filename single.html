Fine Time Los Angeles

Fine Time is an events production company in Los Angeles bringing a variety of cutting edge live and electronic acts to the city. Featured in LA Weekly, Vice, Resident Advisor, and XLR8, Fine Time is on the forefront of the Los Angeles music scene. With that in mind, they were looking for design work which reflected their simultaneously high-end and gritty aesthetic.

On this project, I worked to create event flyers for their Summer event series, as well as merchandise and branding. Starting with rough sketches, the designs were refined through back and forth feedback with Fine Time. A monotype font was used heavily (Inconsolata), giving all the pieces a clean, but industrial feel.
