Limbr

Limbr is a platform for people suffering from depression to find communities and therapists to help them pull out of the vicious cycles that depression often cause. To do so, users are connected with others in a similar situation, in an anonymous, but safe environment in conjunction with the provisioning of coaches and actual therapists. Community + Scaffolding. A great deal of care and consideration was taken to ensure that interactions between users would remain positive, and that private information was kept secure.

In this project, I was responsible for overall feel and UI as well as UX development. I also built out landing pages that collected potential investor information using a Rails backend in conjunction with Mailchimp.
